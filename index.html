Rick and Morty

Rick and Morty is an animated series in the sitcom genre. The creators of Rick and Morty are Justin Roiland and Dan Harmon. Rick and Morty tell about the adventures of Rick and Morty, as well as the Smith family. 5 seasons of this series have been released, and it is not over yet.

Rick

Rick is a genius. He invented a weapon for teleportation, also he can control his organs through technology. Rick can leave his friend in dire situations, but he cares for his family very much. He is also a loth, once when Rick was drunk he wanted to destroy the world.

Morty

Morty is a teenager. Rick uses him as a helper on his adventures. Morty is in love with his classmate (Jessica). Gradually, this teenager grows up and in one episode he even saves Ricky. He often has problems with his family (especially his sister).
