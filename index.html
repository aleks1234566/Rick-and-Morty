Jerry is Morty's father. He is a bit of a fool, Jerry tries to understand his children and have fun with them. Although Jerry is not very intelligent, he is a good man.

About Beth

Beth is Morty's mother. She wants her children to live a normal life. Like all mothers, she wants her children to live well. As time goes on, she faces more and more problems and she can't take the stress any longer.

About Summer

Summer is Morty's older sister, she has a difficult character. While Morty wants a normal life, Summer wants to go on adventures with Rick. She understands Rick very well.
